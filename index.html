What Are Hytale Servers?

Hytale servers are community-run multiplayer servers that allow players to experience Hytale in different ways. Each server offers unique gameplay, including survival worlds, RPG experiences, minigames, creative modes, and custom adventures.

Using our Hytale server list, players can explore the best Hytale servers all in one place. Servers are ranked using real activity, popularity, and player engagement.

By voting for your favourite Hytale servers, you directly support server owners and strengthen the wider Hytale community.
